About Us

A Modern Sushi & Grill Experience in Lisbon

At Prime Sushi & Grill, we believe great food deserves no limits. Our All You Can Eat concept brings together expertly crafted sushi, sashimi, and grilled specialties — made fresh, served fast, and ordered straight from your table.

Every dish is prepared with care using quality ingredients, so whether you're here for a quick lunch or a relaxed dinner with friends, every visit feels generous and unhurried.
